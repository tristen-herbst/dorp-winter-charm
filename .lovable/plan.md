

# Dorp Hotel — Winter Offer Landing Page Mockup

A single-page, visually rich landing page for Dorp's **4-Night Winter Stay** offer (20% off, bookable 01 May – 30 September). Designed to capture international travel intent traffic via SEM campaigns.

## Design Direction
- **Color palette**: Deep charcoal/dark backgrounds with gold (#C5A347) accents — matching Dorp's existing brand
- **Typography**: Elegant serif headings (Playfair Display) with clean sans-serif body text
- **Layout**: Flowing, editorial-style sections with generous whitespace — no boxy grid feel
- **Imagery**: Large atmospheric placeholder images evoking Cape Town winter warmth, gardens, and cozy interiors

## Page Sections (top to bottom)

### 1. Full-Screen Hero
- Full-viewport background image (placeholder) of Dorp's garden/view
- "DORP" wordmark top-left in gold
- Centered overlay text: **"Winter at Dorp"** headline + **"Enjoy 20% Off a 4-Night Stay"** subheadline
- Offer dates: *1 May – 30 September*
- "Book Now" CTA button in gold

### 2. The Offer Details
- Elegant split layout: large image on one side, offer copy on the other
- Key details: 4-night minimum stay, 20% discount, valid dates, direct booking only
- Warm, inviting copy about Cape Town in winter

### 3. Experience Highlights
- 3 overlapping/staggered image cards with captions (not a rigid grid):
  - **Rooms & Suites** — uniquely designed spaces
  - **Dining** — home-style dishes with city views
  - **Gardens & Views** — Signal Hill, Bo-Kaap

### 4. Testimonials Strip
- Horizontal scrolling quotes from VISI, The Daily Telegraph, Inside Guide (pulled from their existing site)
- Subtle gold dividers

### 5. Contact / Enquiry Form
- Elegant form with fields: Name, Email, Country, Message
- "Enquire Now" button
- Contact details alongside: phone, email, address

### 6. Footer
- DORP wordmark, address, social links (Instagram, Facebook)
- Privacy policy link, © 2025

## Responsive
- Fully responsive — mobile-first for international travelers browsing on phones
- Hero image and sections adapt gracefully to smaller screens
